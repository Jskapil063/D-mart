About

Avenue Supermarts Limited, d/b/a DMart, is an Indian retail corporation that operates a chain of supermarkets and hypermarkets. The company was founded by Radhakishan Damani and is based in Mumbai. As of September 2024, DMart has 377 stores across 12 states and union territories in India.[2][1] In March 2024, the company had a total of 13,971 permanent employees and 59,961 employees hired on contractual basis.
